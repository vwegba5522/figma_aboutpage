WHY CBD?

Our choice to incorporate CBD into our products is driven by its remarkable versatility and efficacy. CBD is renowned for its anti-inflammatory properties, making it an ideal ingredient for skincare products aimed at soothing and healing the skin.

Its effectiveness in reducing inflammation is particularly beneficial for conditions like acne, eczema, and psoriasis, aligning with our mission to offer clean, medical-grade skincare solutions.Moreover, CBD's potential in pain relief is a cornerstone of our product development.

By interacting with the body's endocannabinoid system, CBD can help alleviate chronic pain and discomfort, offering a natural alternative to traditional pain medications. This aligns with our goal to provide wellness options that are not only effective but also safe and free from harmful side effects.

CLEAN BEATY & SUSTAINABILITY

Clean beauty, for us, is not just a trend but a core principle. We believe in creating products that are safe, non-toxic, and free from harmful chemicals.

Our 'Free from Terrible 10' policy is a testament to this commitment, ensuring our products are devoid of substances like SLS, parabens, phthalates, and synthetic fragrances, known for their adverse effects on both health and the environment.

This approach extends to every ingredient we choose, ensuring they are 99% natural or naturally derived, hypoallergenic, and dermatologist-tested. By prioritizing ingredients that are as beneficial to the skin as they are harmless to the planet, we embody the true essence of clean beauty.
